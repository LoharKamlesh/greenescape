Essential Travel Tips for India

Local Customs: Respect local traditions and dress modestly, especially when visiting religious sites.
Safety Tips: Stay aware of your surroundings and keep your belongings secure.
Packing List: Include essentials like a power adapter, comfortable shoes, and a reusable water bottle.
Cultural Insights: Learn a few basic phrases in Hindi to enhance your interactions with locals.
